Doritos Recipe

Recipe Description

This crunchy commercial snack can turn even the Grinch's frown upside-down!

Ingredients

  • Credit Card
  • Car (Car Keys)

Steps

  1. Drive to Walmart or to the nearest grocery store.
  2. Look for the snack aisle and put the Doritos bag into your shopping cart.
  3. Buy the Doritos at self-checkout or at the cashier's line using your credit card.
