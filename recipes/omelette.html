Omelette Recipe

Recipe Description

This decadent omelette is a fluffy delicacy that truly highlights the delicious nuances of that signature egg taste! Also, the cook time is short enough for those mornings where you struggle to get out of bed and just need something quick to eat.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • Half-and-half or heavy cream
  • Salt
  • Onion Powder
  • Garlic Powder
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Add oil to the pan and let it heat up over medium-high heat for 3 minutes.
  2. While the pan is heating up, crack the three eggs into a bowl and beat the eggs.
  3. Add three splashes of half-and-half or heavy cream to the mixture along with a pinch of salt and mix.
  4. After the pan is heated, pour the egg mixture into the pan and season with onion and garlic powder.
  5. Once the bottom of the omelette is mostly cooked, flip the omelette to cook the other side.
  6. After the omlette is fully-cooked, turn off the stove and let the eggs cool for two minutes before enjoying!
