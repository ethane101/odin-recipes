Pancakes Recipe

Recipe Description

Warm pancakes are the key to weekends in with family and friends! These pancakes are soft and fluffy and make for a cozy morning.

Ingredients

  • Pancake Mix
  • Water
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Add butter (around 0.5 tbsp) to the pan and let it heat up over medium-high heat for 3 minutes.
  2. Mix the water into the pancake mix.
  3. Cook the pancake batter on the pan and enjoy your pancakes!
